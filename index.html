PRICE IS RIGHT - CARD GAME

Starting at $15,000, guess the price of the car by drawing from a deck of playing cards. If your price is within the given range, you win!
Each card is worth 100 times its value (twos are worth $200, threes are worth $300, etc.). Kings, Jacks and Queens are worth $1,000. Aces are wild: you can make an Ace worth any value in between 0 and $1 less than the current car price, or you could end the game on an Ace by giving an estimated price for the car. You can even end the game without drawing any cards if you feel that the price is already within the range. How many cards do you feel the need to draw to win the game? Good luck!
